This entire website is fully anonymous to support double-blind peer review.

Link to Anonymous PDF paper, as submitted to ISMIR on April 12, 2024.

Really? Yet another MIDI inpainting paper? Yes! Sure, MIDI inpainting's been demonstated many times, but can we make it easier to do, and more expressive? Yes!

In this study, we get comparable performance to prior methods using a Hierarchical Diffusion Transformer (HDiT) that makes things simple and easy, and handles big images (long sequences) too! And we can inpaint unique shapes for our melodies and accompaniments.
